Refund & cancellation policy

Deposits are non-refundable once your request is confirmed. Many manuscripts must be formally requested from library storage in advance — most institutions require a minimum of 7 business days' notice. Once confirmed, we begin that advance coordination immediately.

In plain terms: the moment we confirm your booking, we have already started working for you. Your deposit reflects that commitment.

Payment questions

Why is the deposit non-refundable?

As soon as your request is confirmed we begin advance coordination with the library — including submitting formal pull requests for materials requiring 7 business days' notice. That work cannot be reversed.

What if research takes less than an hour?

Time is billed in full-hour increments, always rounded up. If research takes 20 minutes, you are still billed for one full hour. Your deposit already covers this minimum.

How is the balance invoice delivered?

We send a Stripe invoice to your email address on file at the same time as your scans and research log, payable by card.

What if the record isn't found?

You still owe for time worked and the $10 report fee. Negative evidence — documenting that a record was searched and not found — is a legitimate research outcome and part of the service.
